# Workflow Map

This page maps the main workflows that exist in Nucleus right now.

## Core Mental Model

Most of the app makes more sense if you separate two places:

- the **vault**, which is your long-term library of imported mods and metadata
- the **current MEX project**, which is the active build you are editing and exporting

That distinction is important enough to start with [Vault Vs Project](Vault-Vs-Project.md).

## Short Version

Most actions in Nucleus are one of these:

1. **Import** a thing into the vault.
2. **Install** or apply that thing to the current MEX project.
3. **Export** the current project or package something for sharing.

Character mods, stage variants, extras, CSPs, and patches all follow that general shape, but they differ in what gets stored and what gets written directly into the project.
